Accommodation Recommendation

We recommend staying in one hotel during your trip to avoid moving luggage.

  • Best area: Wangfujing / Dongcheng District
  • Close to major attractions
  • Convenient for restaurants and shopping

Transport

  • Private car service recommended for foreign visitors
  • Easy airport transfer
  • Comfortable travel between attractions

Main Airports:

  • Beijing Capital International Airport (PEK)
  • Beijing Daxing International Airport (PKX)
